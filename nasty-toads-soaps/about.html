About Us

Welcome to Nasty Toad's Swampy Soaps, where nature meets cleanliness! Our business was inspired by the beauty and mystery of the swamp, and we aim to bring a little piece of that magic into your daily routine.

Founded by Nik Turner, our mission is to create eco-friendly, handmade soaps that are as unique as they are refreshing. Each product is crafted with care, using all-natural ingredients and inspired by the scents and sights of the swamp.
