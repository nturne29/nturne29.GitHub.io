Eagle Scout Project

Community Garden Kiosk for Matthews, NC

What's an Eagle Project?

The Eagle Scout Service Project is the opportunity for a Scout to demonstrate leadership of others while performing a project for the benefit of their community.

Planning Phase

Months 1-2

The project included extensive planning sessions with the Town of Matthews' Parks & Rec Committee, utility providers, and local gardeners. I collaborated with a local architect and carpenter to create detailed blueprints.

  • Addressed soil density and support beam depth
  • Designed roof pitch for optimal water runoff
  • Coordinated with utility services

Funding & Logistics

Month 3

Managing this project alongside high school activities was challenging but rewarding. During the planning phase we secured funding through charitable donations and negotiated with local businesses.

  • Obtained materials from Builder's FirstSource and Lowe's
  • Arranged tool rentals and equipment
  • Coordinated volunteer schedules

Project Completion

Month 6

The completed kiosk has been in continuous use since 2018, serving approximately 500 students annually and earning recognition from the local school board.

  • Dedication ceremony with community members
  • Featured in district newsletter
  • Earned Eagle Scout rank (achieved by only 6% of Scouts)
  • Continues to serve as EMS landmark
