Hi! I'm Maxwell Petersen, a 24-year-old senior mechanical engineering technology student at the University of North Carolina - Charlotte (UNCC). My passion for problem-solving and creativity drives my studies and personal projects, particularly in areas such as design and 3D printing.

My role allows me to explore new perspectives daily, perform engaging science demonstrations, teach STEM classes, and develop innovative lab experiences for visitors of all ages.

Whether through my academic pursuits, hands-on projects, or work at Discovery Place Science, I'm always looking for ways to inspire curiosity, foster learning, and tackle challenges with a creative mindset.
